About the Beatrices

International Bakery

Our Story

Everyone loves pastries. But does everyone know pastries? New York City is currently by far the leading metropolitan gateway for immigrants who enter the United States. Lin Beatrice's motto is that “The world will not experience bakery. The world is our bakery.” She grew up in Brooklyn and was surrounded by the diverse communities of New York City. Beatrice’s Bakery was founded upon the idea of making every community in New York City feel at home through a vast menu of international pastries and refreshments.

Quality

Ingredients

Enjoy our state of the art delicacies with the comfort of knowing that we use the finest of ingredients known to humankind. Ingredients are blossomed and imported from countries around the world, including China, Japan, Korea, India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Turkey, Morocco and more!
